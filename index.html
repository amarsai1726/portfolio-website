    Stock Price Prediction

    The Stock Closing Price Prediction project forecasts stock prices using historical data, combining quantitative analysis with sentiment from financial news and social media. It employs time series algorithms like ARIMA, LSTM, and Prophet for stock trends, and classification models such as Random Forest, SVM, and XGBoost to refine predictions. Sentiment analysis, using NLP techniques, adds a qualitative layer by analyzing market sentiment from pre-existing sources. The models are trained on past data, with back-testing to validate accuracy, ensuring reliable predictions. This project helps investors optimize portfolios and manage risk without relying on real-time data.

    Visualizing house prices in North east

    The House Price Visualization project used Tableau to analyze and display real estate trends in the northeastern U.S., focusing on key factors like location, property type, and market trends over time. By designing interactive dashboards with filters for criteria such as region, time period, and price range, the project provided users with an intuitive way to explore housing data. Visualizations like line charts and heat maps highlighted patterns and fluctuations in house prices, offering real estate professionals, buyers, and investors actionable insights to make informed decisions based on clear, data-driven trends.
